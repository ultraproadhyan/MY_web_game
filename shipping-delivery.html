Shipping and Delivery

Delivery of Products

All digital products will be delivered immediately after payment is processed. For physical products, we strive to dispatch your order within 3-5 business days, depending on the shipping method selected.

Shipping Times

Delivery times for physical products vary depending on your location. Typically, orders are delivered within 7-10 business days after dispatch, but this may vary based on the shipping provider and your location.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on your delivery address. The total shipping cost will be displayed at checkout before you complete your order.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email to track the status of your delivery.
